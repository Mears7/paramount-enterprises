Siding

Siding is one of the most noticeable aspects of exteriors, and the Paramount team can help guide you on the big project of updating the exterior of your home or business so you get great results.

Eavestroughs, Soffit, Fascia

Eavestroughs, soffit, and fascia are less noticeable exterior updates, but just as important — proper eavestroughs can actually prevent foundation leaks. They need to be updated every 15 years or so.

Windows and doors

After updating the siding on your house, windows and doors are the next most stunning exterior renovation. Not only will the new windows and doors look great, it will improve your home’s energy efficiency.

Roofing

For both new roofs and re-roofing, Paramount offers a wide range of options for materials, and we keep our sites spotless. If it’s time for some exterior renovations, we’ll come to your house in Saskatoon to give you a quote.
